The Vermont Vernal Pool Atlas

The VPAtlas Project is funded by a State Wildlife Grant from Vermont Fish and Wildlife , with matching grants from the Vermont Watershed Grants Program and the Lintilhac Foundation.

VPAtlas is currently under development in an Alpha product release phase. The data displayed are not final and are not ready for public use. VPAtlas will enter Beta development in the Fall of 2019. During its Beta phase, VPAtlas will be opened to a small group of public users. When VPAtlas reaches its General release, currently slated for 1/1/2020, all public features of VPAtlas will become available.

The Vernal Pool Atlas is a project to map and to monitor Vernal Pools in Vermont. VPAtlas combines all Vermont Vernal Pool data into a single mapping database, including data from the Vernal Pool Mapping Project and the Vernal Pool Monitoring Project . Also new with VPAtlas is an online interface designed to gather data from citizen scientists and the public.
