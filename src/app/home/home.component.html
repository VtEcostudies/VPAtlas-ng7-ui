The Vermont Vernal Pool Atlas

The Vermont Vernal Pool Atlas is an online, interactive database that compiles and archives data for the Vernal Pool Mapping Project and the Vernal Pool Monitoring Project. Everyone is welcome to browse the Atlas using the 'Search Pools' tab in the menu bar. In order to submit pool locations or data from pool visits, you must first Register and create a Username and Password. Once you login the 'Add Pool Data' tab will be visible.

VPAtlas is currently under development in Beta release. The data displayed are reviewed by VPAtlas administrators on a regular basis. Beta development will continue in 2020 and is expected to complete by the end of 2020.

The VPAtlas Project is funded by a State Wildlife Grant from Vermont Fish & Wildlife , with matching grants from the Lintilhac Foundation and the Vermont Watershed Grants Program.
