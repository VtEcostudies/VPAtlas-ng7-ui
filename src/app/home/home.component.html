This site is an Alpha version of VPAtlas, the Vermont Vernal Pool Atlas Project. VPAtlas is a joint effort of the Vermont Center for Ecostudies and Vermont Fish and Wildlife. The VPAtlas Project is funded by the Watershed Grants Program, and the Lintillac Foundation.

The Vernal Pool Atlas is a project to map and monitor Vernal Pools in Vermont. VPAtlas combines all Vermont Vernal Pool data into a single mapping database, including data from the Vernal Pool Mapping Project and the Vernal Pool Monitoring Project. Also new with VPAtlas is an online interface designed to gather data from citizen scientists and the public. VPAtlas data are free and available for public consumption.
